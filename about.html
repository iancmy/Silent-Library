Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To provide quality and sufficient information that addresses the shifting needs of all kinds of people. In a friendly and encouraging environment, Silent Library encourages and preserves universal access to a wide spectrum of human knowledge, experience, information, and ideas.

Where we came from

Silent Library started back in 2017 in a small rental place which barely fits 10 people at a time with just about 500 books. Today, we can accomodate up to 1000 people at a time and with over 10,000 books available!
